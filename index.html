Questions

Everything you might wonder.

If your question isn't here, reach out at contact@lotngo.com — we respond fast.

Why not just use our building's WhatsApp group? ▾
Because WhatsApp groups mean noise and chaos. One request spams everyone’s phone, meaning most people mute the group entirely. Plus, many residents hate publicly begging for favors, and offers get buried under chat history in minutes.

Lot'N'Go brings total silence. You open the app, find a spot, claim it in two clicks, and park—no group spam, no social awkwardness, and perfect order.
What can I do with the App? ▾
Lot'N'Go lets you share, find, and book parking spots with your building's residents. Mark your spot available when you're away, get notified when neighbors need parking, and reserve a free spot instantly when you have guests or need temporary parking. Everything is managed through your smartphone — no app store required.
What are the advantages for Residents? ▾
Park stress-free. Share your spot when you're away and help neighbors. Find a free spot in seconds when you need one. No money changes hands, no awkward conversations — just good neighboring made simple."
What are the advantages for Building Managers & HOAs? ▾
Less conflict, happier residents, zero management headache. Reduce parking disputes before they start. Show your community you care with a modern, thoughtful amenity. The system runs itself — you just activate it once.
How does my building join? ▾
The building manager or HOA representative signs up via lotngo.com and registers the building's marked spots. Residents are then invited by unit number. The whole setup takes under 30 minutes.
What if someone doesn't vacate the spot on time? ▾
The app sends automatic reminders 15 minutes before the window ends. In rare cases, the spot owner can flag a situation through the app, and the building manager is notified instantly.
Is it only for private residential buildings? ▾
Currently yes — Lot'N'Go is designed for residential buildings with assigned private parking spots. Office buildings and commercial lots are not yet supported, but they're on the roadmap.
How is it accessible? ▾
The app is built so it can run directly from your smartphone web browser with no need for an app store. It's simpler, secure and always up to date.
How secure is it? ▾
A resident can only register for their building after the building admin enters their phone number, so they need to be in contact beforehand. This ensures only legitimate residents have access.
