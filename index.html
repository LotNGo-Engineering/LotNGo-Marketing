How does a guest or technician physically enter through the building’s main gate? ▾
The app handles scheduling and spot coordination purely digitally. To open the electric gate, the guest or driver will contact the resident who invited them or reserved the spot for entrance (via intercom, phone call, or remote clicker).
What happens if someone doesn't vacate my spot on time? ▾
The system fires automatic countdown reminders 15 minutes before any scheduled window ends to ensure people leave on time. In rare cases where a spot isn't vacated when it should be, the owner can handle it through standard building channels (such as notifying neighbors in the building's WhatsApp group, contacting building management, or calling local city supervision / police if necessary).
