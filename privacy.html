Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 — This policy applies to the Lot'N'Go website (lotngo.com) and its future application.

Lot'N'Go is currently in pre-launch. The application is under development and no user accounts, parking data, or personal profiles are collected at this time. The only data we may collect is your email address if you voluntarily join our waitlist.

1. Who We Are

Lot'N'Go operates the website lotngo.com and is developing a parking-sharing platform for residential buildings. For any privacy-related inquiries, contact us at contact@lotngo.com.

2. What Data We Collect

At this pre-launch stage, we collect only:

  • Your email address, if you choose to join the waitlist.
  • Basic technical data (browser type, page visit) via standard web server logs. This data is not linked to your identity.

We do not collect location data, payment data, or any sensitive personal information at this stage.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your email address, if provided, is used solely to notify you when Lot'N'Go launches. We will not send marketing emails, share your address with third parties, or use it for any other purpose without your explicit consent.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

This policy is governed by the Israeli Privacy Protection Law, 5741–1981 and its regulations, and is designed to align with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Union.

The legal basis for processing your email (where applicable under GDPR) is your freely given, specific, and informed consent at the point of submission.

5. Your Rights

Regardless of your location, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data at any time.
  • Withdraw consent for processing (e.g. remove yourself from the waitlist).
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority in your country.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at contact@lotngo.com.

6. Cookies

The Lot'N'Go website does not currently use tracking cookies, analytics tools, or advertising scripts. If this changes in future, this policy will be updated and users will be notified.

7. Data Retention

Waitlist email addresses are retained until the service launches or until you request removal, whichever comes first. Server logs are retained for a maximum of 30 days.

8. Changes to This Policy

As Lot'N'Go develops and launches, this policy will be updated to reflect new data practices. Material changes will be communicated via email to waitlist subscribers.
