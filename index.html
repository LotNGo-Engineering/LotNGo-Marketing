Questions

Everything you might wonder.

If your question isn't here, reach out at contact@lotngo.com — we respond fast.

How does pricing work?▾
Lot'N'Go is billed to the building management company or HOA — not to individual residents. Pricing is a low monthly fee per apartment or parking spot, with a discounted yearly plan available. This covers the platform, servers, and support. Residents use the app at no personal cost.
How does my building join?▾
The building manager or HOA representative signs up via lotngo.com and registers the building's marked spots. Residents are then invited by unit number. The whole setup takes under 30 minutes.
What if someone doesn't vacate the spot on time?▾
The app sends automatic reminders 15 minutes before the window ends. In rare cases, the spot owner can flag a situation through the app, and the building manager is notified instantly.
Is it only for private residential buildings?▾
Currently yes — Lot'N'Go is designed for residential buildings with assigned private parking spots. Office buildings and commercial lots are not yet supported, but they're on the roadmap.
How is it accessible?▾
The app is built so it can run directly from your smartphone web browser with no need for an app store. It's simpler, secure and always up to date.
